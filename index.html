What We Do

We set it up, train your overseers or deploy our own trained coordinators, and hand you a living system that replaces manual reporting with real-time progress visibility across all your partner organisations.

Proof of Execution
Already running.
Already working.

Green Beacons is not a concept. It is a live system currently managing over 1,000 participants across 12 partner organisations under the Social Employment Fund — piloted within DUCT (Social Employment Fund), KwaZulu-Natal.

The system is currently in discussion with the Industrial Development Corporation (IDC) for broader deployment, and in active conversation with Wild Trust for their YES Nedbank intern programme.
